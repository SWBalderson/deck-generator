# Q4 2024 Strategic Review

## Executive Summary

Our Q4 performance exceeded targets by 15%, driven primarily by expansion in the European market. Revenue reached £2.4M against a target of £2.1M.

## Key Achievements

- **Revenue Growth**: 15% above target (£2.4M vs £2.1M)
- **New Client Acquisition**: 12 new enterprise clients
- **Market Expansion**: Successfully launched in Germany and France
- **Operational Efficiency**: Reduced delivery time by 23%

## Challenges

- Supply chain disruptions affected 8% of orders
- Staff turnover in Sales team reached 18%

## Q1 2025 Priorities

1. Consolidate European presence
2. Address supply chain vulnerabilities
3. Implement retention strategy for Sales team
